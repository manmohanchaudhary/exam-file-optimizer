## **Eligibility Criteria for NEET PG 2026**

Before filling out the form, ensure you meet the strict eligibility requirements set by the National Medical Commission (NMC).

1. **Educational Qualification:** Candidates must possess a recognized MBBS degree or a provisional pass certificate.
2. **Medical Registration:** A valid permanent or provisional registration certificate issued by the NMC or State Medical Council (SMC) is mandatory.
3. **Internship Completion:** This is the most crucial factor. For the 2026 session, you must complete your one-year compulsory rotatory internship on or before **September 30, 2026**.
4. **No Attempt Limit:** There is currently no restriction on the number of attempts or an upper age limit for the exam.

## **Step-by-Step Guide to Filling the NEET PG 2026 Form**

Follow these steps to complete your registration accurately:

### **Step 1: User Registration**

Visit the official portal at **natboard.edu.in**. Click on "New Registration" and enter your name, date of birth, mobile number, and email ID. You will receive a unique **User ID and Password** via SMS and email.

### **Step 2: Filling Academic & Personal Details**

Login with your credentials. Enter your personal info, academic scores (MBBS marks), and specific internship start and end dates.

### **Step 3: Test City Selection**

Choose your preferred examination city. Allocation is on a **first-come, first-served basis**. If a city reaches capacity, you must select from the remaining available options.

### **Step 4: Uploading Digital Assets**

Upload your recent photo, signature, and thumb impression. In 2026, some portals may also require a **live photograph** capture during the process for identity verification.

### **Step 5: Fee Payment & Confirmation**

Pay the category-wise fee online. Once successful, a **Confirmation Page** will be generated. Download and print at least two copies for your records.

## **Correction Window and Non-Editable Fields**

NBEMS typically provides a brief window to correct minor errors. However, the following fields are **strictly non-editable**:

* Name of the Candidate
* Email ID
* Mobile Number
* Nationality
* Test City Preference

Editable fields usually include date of birth, gender, category, and academic qualifications.
