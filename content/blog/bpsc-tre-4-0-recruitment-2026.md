The Bihar Public Service Commission (BPSC) has initiated the fourth phase of its massive teacher recruitment drive, known as TRE 4.0, for the 2026 academic cycle. This recruitment is a cornerstone of Bihar’s educational reforms, aiming to fill approximately 46,595 teaching positions across primary, middle, secondary, and senior secondary levels. With a modernized application process involving One-Time Registration (OTR) and DigiLocker integration, TRE 4.0 represents one of the most transparent and efficient hiring cycles in the state's history.

## BPSC TRE 4.0 Exam 2026: Key Highlights and Dates

The BPSC has released a revised annual calendar and short notices to provide a roadmap for aspirants. The recruitment process is scheduled to conclude within the 2026 calendar year to ensure new teachers are stationed before the next academic session.

| Official Detailed Notification (PDF) | Expected March 2026 |

| Admit Card Availability | September 2026 (2-3 weeks before exam) |

## Eligibility Criteria for BPSC TRE 4.0 2026

Candidates must meet specific educational and professional standards set by the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE).

### 1. Primary Teacher (Classes 1–5)
- **Education**: Senior Secondary (10+2) with at least 50% marks and a 2-year Diploma in Elementary Education (D.El.Ed) or 4-year B.El.Ed.
- **Eligibility Test**: Must have qualified CTET Paper 1 or BTET Paper 1.

### 2. Middle School Teacher (Classes 6–8)
- **Education**: Graduation with a 2-year D.El.Ed or B.Ed/B.Ed Special Education.
- **Eligibility Test**: Must have qualified CTET Paper 2 or BTET Paper 2.

### 3. Secondary (TGT) and Senior Secondary (PGT)
- **TGT (9–10)**: Graduation/PG in the relevant subject with 50% marks + B.Ed + STET Paper 1.
- **PGT (11–12)**: Master’s degree in the relevant subject with 50% marks + B.Ed + STET Paper 2.
- **Specialized Subjects**: Computer Science roles require B.E/B.Tech (CS/IT), MCA, or M.Sc (CS) with at least 50% marks.

## Step-by-Step OTR and Application Process

For the 2026 cycle, BPSC has made One-Time Registration (OTR) mandatory to create a permanent candidate profile.

1. **OTR Registration**: Visit onlinebpsc.bihar.gov.in and select the OTR link.
2. **DigiLocker Sync**: Log in to your DigiLocker to upload educational certificates (10th, 12th, B.Ed). During OTR, use the "Fetch from DigiLocker" option to auto-verify documents.
3. **Create Profile**: Enter identity details and verify via OTP sent to your Aadhaar-linked mobile number.
4. **Post Selection**: Once OTR is locked, click "Apply Online" for TRE 4.0 and select your level (PRT/Middle/TGT/PGT).
5. **Fee Payment**: Pay the application fee (typically ₹100 for Bihar categories; up to ₹750 for others).
6. **Confirmation**: Download and print the final application form for document verification.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

**Q1:** Is there any negative marking in BPSC TRE 4.0?  
**Answer:** No, the commission has confirmed that there is no negative marking for this recruitment cycle.

**Q2:** Can candidates from other states apply for Bihar Teacher jobs?  
**Answer:** Yes, candidates from other states are eligible to apply under the General category, though they will not be eligible for reservation benefits.

**Q3:** What are the minimum qualifying marks?  
**Answer:** The minimum threshold is 40% for the General category, 36.5% for BC, 34% for EBC, and 32% for SC/ST and Female candidates.

**Q4:** Is B.Ed mandatory for Primary Teacher (PRT) posts?  
**Answer:** For PRT (Classes 1–5), D.El.Ed is the primary requirement. B.Ed candidates should check the detailed March notification for the latest NCTE-aligned rules.

**Q5:** What documents are needed for OTR?  
**Answer:** You need your Aadhaar card, matriculation certificate (for DOB), degree mark sheets, and teacher training certificates (B.Ed/D.El.Ed).
