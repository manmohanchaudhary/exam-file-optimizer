Indian Railways has released the **RRB Group D Recruitment Notification 2026 under Centralised Employment Notice (CEN) 09/2025**. This recruitment drive offers thousands of vacancies in Level-1 posts across multiple railway zones in India.

Candidates who have passed **Class 10th or ITI** can apply for these positions through the official Railway Recruitment Board portal.

:::note
The official notification is available at: [rrbapply.gov.in](https://www.rrbapply.gov.in/assets/forms/CEN_09_2025_level_1.pdf)
:::

---

## Overview of RRB Group D 2026 Recruitment

The RRB Group D recruitment is one of the largest government job recruitments conducted by Indian Railways every year.

Key highlights:

* **Organization:** Railway Recruitment Board (RRB)
* **Notification Number:** CEN 09/2025
* **Posts:** Level-1 (Group D)
* **Vacancies:** Approximately 22,000+ posts
* **Application Mode:** Online

:::tip
Start preparing your documents early! You will need a recent passport-size photograph and a scanned signature that meet strict RRB guidelines.
:::

## Eligibility Criteria

### Educational Qualification

Candidates must have one of the following:

* Class 10 (Matriculation) from a recognized board
* ITI from NCVT/SCVT recognized institute

### Age Limit

* **Minimum Age:** 18 years
* **Maximum Age:** 33 years

Age relaxation is applicable for reserved categories as per government rules.

---

## Application Fee

* **General / OBC / EWS:** ₹500
* **SC / ST / Female / PwBD / Ex-Servicemen / Minorities:** ₹250

Part of the fee may be refunded after appearing for the CBT exam as per notification guidelines.

---

## Selection Process

The recruitment process includes multiple stages:

:::step{number="1" title="Computer Based Test (CBT)"}
The first stage is a computer-based test covering Mathematics, General Intelligence, General Science, and General Awareness.
:::

:::step{number="2" title="Physical Efficiency Test (PET)"}
Candidates who clear the CBT will be called for a physical test which includes running and carrying weight.
:::

:::step{number="3" title="Document Verification"}
Verification of all original educational and identity documents.
:::

:::step{number="4" title="Medical Examination"}
A strict medical fitness test as per railway standards.
:::

## RRB Photo and Signature Requirements

When applying online, you must upload your photograph and signature in the exact format specified by RRB.

:::warning
Applications with blurred photos, photos with caps/sunglasses, or incorrect dimensions will be **rejected immediately**.
:::

### Photo Requirements:
* Size: 20KB to 50KB
* Dimensions: 35mm x 45mm
* Format: JPEG/JPG
* Background: White or light color

### Signature Requirements:
* Size: 10KB to 40KB
* Dimensions: 50mm x 20mm
* Format: JPEG/JPG
* Ink: Black or Blue pen on white paper
