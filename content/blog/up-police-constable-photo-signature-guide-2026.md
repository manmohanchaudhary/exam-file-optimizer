## Critical "Do Not Reject" Rules for UP Police

To guarantee your application is accepted, you must strictly adhere to these rules:

* **No Spectacles:** Even if you wear glasses daily for vision correction, you must remove them for the photograph. Glare on lenses is a primary reason for rejection.
* **Face Coverage:** Your face should be clearly visible and must cover at least **70%** of the total photo area.
* **No Selfies:** Mobile selfies are strictly prohibited. Always use a professional, studio-style passport photograph.
* **Neutral Expression:** Look straight into the camera with a neutral expression. No smiling, frowning, or showing teeth.

**Q: Does the photo need a date and name printed on it?**
A: For UP Police Constable, printing the name and date on the photo is generally not mandatory unless specifically stated in the latest notification. However, the photo must be recent (taken within the last 6 months).

**Q: Can I use a blue background for my photo?**
