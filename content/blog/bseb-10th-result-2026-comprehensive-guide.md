The Bihar School Examination Board (BSEB) has officially declared the Class 10 Matric results for 2026 today, March 29, at approximately 1:15 PM IST following a press conference in Patna. This announcement marks the culmination of exams held in February, affecting over 15 lakh students across the state. With an impressive overall pass percentage of 81.79%, the results highlight strong academic performance amid rigorous evaluations.

## Official Release Details
BSEB Chairman Anand Kishor unveiled the outcomes, including toppers and statistics, during the live event. Results went live shortly after on key portals, though high traffic caused brief delays. Exams spanned February 17 to 25 in two daily shifts at 1,699 centers statewide.

## Direct Result Links
Access your scorecard instantly using roll code and number—no password required initially:

*   **Matric Portal:** [matricbiharboard.com](http://matricbiharboard.com)

**Steps:** Log in > Enter details > View marksheet > Download PDF. Provisional copies are valid for admissions; originals reach schools within weeks.

## Toppers List Highlights
After verification, joint first rank goes to Pushpanjali Kumari from Simultala Awasiya Vidyalaya, Jamui, and Sabreen Parveen (or Parveen) from Uch Madhyamik Vidyalaya, Vaishali, both with 492 marks (98.4%). Full district-wise lists feature high performers from Patna, Gaya, and Muzaffarpur. Key ranks:

*   **1st:** Pushpanjali Kumari (Jamui), Sabreen Parveen (Vaishali) – 492 marks
*   **2nd-5th:** Scores 488–490 from Patna and Nalanda (full list on portals)

**Top 10 Prizes:** Rs 2 lakh for 1st, Rs 1 lakh for 2nd/3rd

Detailed toppers available on BSEB sites post-verification.

## How to Check and Download Results Step-by-Step
1.  Visit any official link above.
2.  Select "Matric Result 2026."
3.  Input roll code (4 digits) and roll number (6–7 digits).
4.  Submit; view subject-wise marks out of 100 each (total 500).
5.  Download/share web marksheet; print for records.

**Pro Tip:** Use multiple devices or incognito mode if sites slow down.

## What’s Next: Post-Result Processes
*   **Compartment/Supplementary:** Apply May 2026 for failed subjects.
*   **Re-totalling/Scrutiny:** Within 10–15 days; fee Rs 100/subject.
*   **Admissions:** Class 11 via UGEAC portal (Science/Arts/Commerce streams).
*   **Original Marksheets:** School distribution from April 10.
*   **Migration/TC:** For CBSE/ICSE shifts, apply online.

## Background on BSEB Matric Exams
BSEB conducts annual board exams for Bihar's secondary education, emphasizing bilingual papers (Hindi/English mediums). 2026 syllabus aligned with NCERT, focusing 40% competency-based questions. Evaluations involved external examiners with strict anti-cheating measures like CCTV.
